Early Research

Since undergraduate I have been involved in the development and design of multiple investigations in different fields such as cellular damage and protection evidenced in genes or genotoxicity, diagnosis through molecular tests and analysis of genetic and genomic information and its implication in epidemiological outbreak control.

Some of this projects:

  • Evaluation of the genotoxicity and antigenotoxicity of Vasconcellea candicans by Somatic Mutation and Recombination Test (SMART).
  • Molecular cloning of the genome of the avian infectious bronchitis coronavirus.
  • Standardization and design of Real-Time PCR multiplex for clinical diagnostics.
